## üìù Video Recording Tips

### Technical Setup:
- **Camera:** Eye-level, HD quality (1080p minimum)
- **Lighting:** Soft, even lighting on your face (ring light or natural window light)
- **Audio:** Use external microphone or quality headset (test beforehand!)
- **Background:** Clean, professional, minimal distractions
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders visible, slight space above head
- **Recording software:** OBS Studio, Zoom, or PowerPoint recording feature

### Delivery Guidance for Video:
- **Look at camera** ‚Äì Not at slides or your own image. This creates connection with viewers
- **Energy level** ‚Äì Slightly higher than normal conversation (video can feel flat)
- **Use vocal variety** ‚Äì Emphasize key points with pace and tone changes
- **Strategic pauses** ‚Äì Let critical statistics sink in (e.g., "two to three quarters" - pause 2 seconds)
- **Gesture naturally** ‚Äì Hands visible occasionally adds engagement
- **Smile when appropriate** ‚Äì Especially during solution sections (Slide 3-5)
- **No filler words** ‚Äì Edit out "ums" and "ahs" if recording in segments

### Recording Strategy:
- **Option 1: Single take** ‚Äì Record entire 12-15 minute video in one go (have backup takes ready)
- **Option 2: Segment recording** ‚Äì Record each slide section separately, edit together
- **Recommended: Hybrid** ‚Äì Record in 2-3 segments (Problem/Idea, How it Works/Innovation, Value/Timeline/Close)

### Post-Production:
- **Add text overlays** for key statistics ("2-3 quarters", "Under 3 seconds", "$600/month")
- **Transitions** between slides (simple, professional)
- **Lower third** with your name/title at opening
- **End screen** with contact info or next steps
- **Closed captions** if possible (accessibility + professionalism)

### B-Roll Suggestions (Optional but Impactful):
- Screen recording of similar query tools for comparison
- Animated diagram of how VitalsIQ works
- Mock-up screenshots of the proposed interface
- Quick cuts to relevant slides during technical explanations

### Common Video Recording Mistakes to Avoid:
- ‚ùå Reading from script (use bullet points, speak naturally)
- ‚ùå Monotone delivery (vary your pace and energy)
- ‚ùå Looking down at notes constantly
- ‚ùå Poor audio quality (this kills credibility fast)
- ‚ùå Too long - keep within 15 minutes maximum
- ‚ùå Forgetting a strong opening and closing

### Final Checklist Before Submitting:
- ‚úÖ Video length: 12-15 minutes (not over 15!)
- ‚úÖ Audio is clear and consistent throughout
- ‚úÖ All slides are visible and legible
- ‚úÖ You look at camera during key moments
- ‚úÖ Energy is maintained throughout (re-record if you sound tired)
